MSG Field Guide

By Jack Settleman ✓ ·New York Knicks·New York, NY
9.2
Snapback · #3

There's no building in sports like the Garden. Follow the seven steps below in order — arrive right, sit right, eat right — and a Knicks night becomes the best crowd in the NBA.

1
Outside
Get there
Best ways in: walking, the subway, the LIRR, or NJ Transit. Driving would be my last choice.
Jack Settleman ✓
2
Gates
Get in
Come in through the 100 level gates — the merch store there has a way shorter line than the one in the MSG lobby.
Jack Settleman ✓
3
Chase Lounge
Pregame
There's a Chase reserve lounge inside the arena you can book pregame. Nearby: Nick and Stef's, Stout, and Mustang Harry's.
Jack Settleman ✓
4
100 Level
Find your seat
Front rows in the 200s in the center sections beat behind the basket, even down low. The Hyundai Bridge is a cool spot and good value.
Jack Settleman ✓
5
Steak Sandwich
Eat
The best food in MSG is the steak sandwich outside section 107. The Garden Market hamburgers are the best basic food in sports. Skip the pizza.
Jack Settleman ✓
6
Tip-off
Soak it in
Best crowd in the NBA. Be ready to stand, and the seats are tight — but there's plenty of in-game entertainment like t-shirt tosses.
Jack Settleman ✓
7
Team Store
On the way out
Grab merch through the 100 level gates for the shorter line. One heads-up: alcoholic drinks are very expensive inside MSG — budget for it.
Jack Settleman ✓
