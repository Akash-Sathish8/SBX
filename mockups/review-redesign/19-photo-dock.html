There's no building in sports like the Garden. Get there right, sit in the right seat, eat the right sandwich, and a Knicks night becomes the best crowd in the NBA. Here's exactly how to do it — and the photo strip below keeps pace with whatever you're reading.

Getting in

Arrive · gates
Jack Settleman ✓

Best ways in: walking, the subway, the LIRR, or NJ Transit. Driving would be my last choice. The building sits right on top of Penn Station, so transit drops you at the door.

38

When you get to the doors, come in through the 100 level gates — the merch store there has a way shorter line than the one in the MSG lobby.

31

Before the game

Pregame · lounges
Jack Settleman ✓

Pregame spots nearby: Nick and Stef's Steakhouse, The Rutherford, Stout, Mustang Harry's, and American Whiskey. All an easy walk from the gates.

22

There's a Chase reserve lounge inside the arena — you can reserve a spot pregame and skip the concourse crush.

15

Best seats

Where to sit
Jack Settleman ✓

Front rows in the 200s in the center sections beat behind the basket, even down low. It's the sweet spot for the price.

41

The Chase Bridge and the Hyundai Bridge are both cool spots, and the Hyundai Bridge is good value.

33

The Delta Club comes with all-inclusive food and drink (non-alcoholic) if you want to go all-in.

19
4

Best food

What to eat
Jack Settleman ✓

Best food in MSG is the steak sandwich outside section 107. Non-negotiable — get there early, the line builds.

57

The hamburgers from the Garden Market are the best basic food item in sports. They use a different type of bun that is fantastic.

44

Carnegie Deli is another good one. Skip the pizza — it is bad in the arena.

28
5

Atmosphere

The crowd
Jack Settleman ✓

Best crowd in the NBA. Be ready to stand, and the seats are tight between people. There are not as many gimmicks as other NBA arenas, so it's not the most family-friendly — but there's still plenty of in-game entertainment like t-shirt tosses. The energy is worth it.

63
6

Insider tips

Know before you go
Jack Settleman ✓

The merch store through the gates on the 100 level has a way shorter line than the one in the MSG lobby.

31

Alcoholic drinks are very expensive inside MSG. Budget for it, or handle it at a bar pregame.
