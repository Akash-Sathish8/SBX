How to read this

Team matchups are draw-dependent. Match counts, dates and round types are verified; specific opponents come from secondary aggregators and must be reconfirmed against fifa.com.

Capacities vary by source; some WC seating configs were never published (Atlanta, Boston, Philadelphia).

Fan-festival models differ — most cities have one official site, but the SF Bay Area and Seattle are distributed (no single hub).

Bag policy is tournament-wide (FIFA clear-bag ≤12″×6″×12″ + small clutch). Bars/supporter lists are travel-media and affiliations can shift. Transit plans finalize close to matchday.
